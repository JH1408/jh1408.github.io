About me

Hi, I’m Josy,

a self-taught Software Engineer and Engineering Manager with a passion for building user-centric products in fast-paced environments. I have a proven track record in leading high-impact projects, building and scaling teams, and collaborating closely with Product Managers and Designers to deliver innovative solutions.

My journey into tech began after working as a pharmacist, where I realized I wanted a more flexible lifestyle. A short introductory HTML course ignited my love for coding, leading me to make the leap into software development. Since then, I’ve developed a solid technical foundation, evolving from writing my first lines of code to leading engineering teams and delivering high-impact projects. My hands-on approach and commitment to continuous learning have allowed me to grow rapidly in this field, where I now thrive in fast-paced environments, driving innovation and collaboration. I’m excited to continue building products that make a difference while embracing new challenges and opportunities for growth.

Some more about me: I’m a positive-thinking scuba diver, traveler, foodie and dog lover from Germany.
